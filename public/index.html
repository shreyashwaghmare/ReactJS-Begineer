Street Marketing

Auto rickshaw marketing, also known as auto branding, is a form of transit media advertising that involves pasting flex posters on auto-rickshaws. It's one of the oldest forms of advertising and is a form of mass advertising. Marketing for business is a highly valuable tool as it helps to create brand awareness, drive profit and growth, acquire and retain customers, and enhance engagement. Effective marketing strategies play a pivotal role in establishing a strong presence in the market and connecting with the target audience.
